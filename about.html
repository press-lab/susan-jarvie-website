About Susan

E-RYT500 Yoga Instructor · Mesa, Arizona

About

Join me on your yoga journey

you matter!

I'm a registered yoga instructor (E-RYT500, Yoga Alliance, since 2006) and adjunct faculty member in the Exercise Science department at Mesa Community College. I've led group fitness yoga sessions since 2008, and my practice spans over two decades.

My teaching draws from physical alignment, mindful sequencing, and breath awareness — bringing together postures, breathing techniques, meditation, and visualization so each class nourishes body and mind.

Philosophy

My Teaching Approach

Classes are designed to be accessible to all levels and ages. Whether you're stepping onto a mat for the first time or deepening a long-standing practice, I meet you where you are. Every session incorporates:

  • ✦ Physical alignment and posture guidance
  • ✦ Mindful breathing and pranayama
  • ✦ Meditation and visualization
  • ✦ A warm, welcoming community
