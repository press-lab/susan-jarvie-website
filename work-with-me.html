One-on-One

Private Yoga Sessions

Your individual needs and goals can be met with private yoga study. My one-to-one sessions are tailored entirely to you — your body, your pace, and the gifts yoga has to offer: purification of body and mind, clarity, and a calm, energized spirit.

Regular private practice helps you feel more comfortable in your body, build strength and flexibility, and develop tools for stress and life's busy moments.

Sessions include:

  • Personalized posture and alignment guidance
  • Breathing techniques (pranayama)
  • Meditation and visualization
  • Guidance for beginners through intermediate practitioners

60 and 90-minute sessions available at my home studio or at your home.
