About the Classes

Hatha Flow

Hatha Yoga promotes overall health by strengthening muscles and stimulating the glands and organs. Classes include basic postures, breathing techniques, and relaxation.

Gentle / Restorative Yoga

Gentle yoga is a slower, more accessible way to practice, appropriate for all ages and fitness levels. A wonderful entry point into yoga.
