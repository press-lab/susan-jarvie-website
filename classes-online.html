Classes Online

Practice yoga with me from anywhere — two easy ways to join.

Whether you prefer a structured course with assignments and community, or the freedom to drop in whenever you want, I have an option for you.

Structured Course

Mesa Community College — Online & Recorded

Register through Mesa Community College for classes that meet online and are recorded for you to revisit anytime. A two-credit introductory course covering yoga history, philosophy, postures, and breath work.

Cost: Under $100/semester (≈ $3/class)
Register at mesacc.edu

Actual classes filmed on location with students, so you get the real experience of being in the room. Subscribe so you never miss a new class — and remember: the more you watch and comment, the bigger our community grows!
